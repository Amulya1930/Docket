Kitchen Cleaning Checklist

  • Packup and put away any leftover food
  • Empty Dishwasher
  • Load Dirty dishes into dishwasher
  • wash large dishes and pots by hand
  • put dirty napkins and placemats in laundry
    • >Put away clean placemats and napkins
  • wipe counters and table
  • push in chairs
  • rinse out the sink
  • take out the garbage
  • dry and put away any clean dishes and pots
  • wipe any spills off of cabinet doors
  • put kitchen towel and dishcloth in laundry; set put new ones
  • put away any toys and clutter
  • clean front of fridge stove-front and dishwasher
  • If stovetop is cool, wipe it down
  • Shake throw rugs and vacuum kitchen
