Back-To-School Shopping Checklist

  • Backpacks, personalized or with name tags
  • Lunch kits and water bottles, personalized or with name tags
  • Notebooks, folders and binders
  • Staplers and fasteners
  • Art smocks, uniforms and uniform accessories including hair ties, socks, hats and shoes
  • Non-toxic crayons, colored pens, washable markers and highlighters
  • Index cards and sticky notes
  • Pencil cases
  • Pens, pencils, erasers and rulers
  • Umbrellas and lightweight foldable waterproof coats
  • USB flash drives
  • Tablets, e-readers, netbooks or computers
  • Packaged healthy snacks
